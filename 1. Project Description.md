# Zomato Customer and Outlet Churn Analytics

---

# Overview

This project analyzes the performance of Zomato, a food delivery marketplace from October 2017 to June 2020 to identify the key factors driving a decline in sales.

Using Tableau dashboards and business analytics techniques, the project examines sales trends, user behavior, outlet performance, cuisine distribution, and city-level performance. The analysis focuses on uncovering the root cause behind revenue decline and providing strategic recommendations to restore growth.

---

# Problem Statement

The food delivery platform experienced a consistent decline in sales despite maintaining stable customer satisfaction and spending metrics.

The objective was to answer the following business questions:

- Why are sales declining over time?
- Is customer spending decreasing?
- Are specific cities contributing more to the decline?
- Which cuisines and restaurants are most impacted?
- What business actions can reverse the trend?

# Key Insights

## 1. Sales Decline

- Sales showed a continuous month-over-month decline.
- The latest month recorded approximately **42% lower sales** compared to October 2017.

## 2. Customer Spending Remained Stable

Despite declining sales:

- Median Rating remained above **4**
- Average User Value (AUV) remained around **12K**

This indicates customers were not spending less.

## 3. User Churn Was Driving Revenue Loss

- User decline closely mirrored sales decline.
- AUV remained stable throughout the period.
- Revenue decline was primarily caused by customer churn rather than reduced spending.

## 4. City-Level Trends Differed Significantly

Example:

- Bangalore recorded approximately **97% growth** in sales compared to October 2017.

This suggests the decline was concentrated in specific cities rather than being platform-wide.

## 5. User Distribution Shifted Across Cities

- Demographic cohorts declined at similar rates.
- User mix across cities changed significantly.
- Certain cities experienced disproportionate customer loss.

## 6. Outlet Closures Were the Root Cause

A strong correlation was observed between outlet decline and user decline.

**Key Finding:** Users were leaving because restaurants were leaving the platform.

Outlet churn emerged as the primary driver of customer churn and sales decline.

## 7. Delhi Was the Most Impacted Major Market

Among major cities:

- Delhi experienced approximately **36% outlet decline**

which contributed significantly to user and revenue loss.

## 8. Cuisine Performance

The largest declines were observed in:

- North Indian Cuisine
- Chinese Cuisine
- Indian Cuisine

These categories declined by approximately **50%** during the analysis period.

# Results & Conclusion

The analysis revealed that declining sales were not caused by lower customer spending or poor customer experience.

### Key Findings

- Customer ratings remained stable.
- Average User Value remained stable.
- User churn was the primary driver of sales decline.
- User churn was directly linked to outlet closures.
- Certain cities, especially Delhi, experienced severe outlet decline.
- North Indian, Chinese, and Indian cuisines were the most affected categories.

### Business Conclusion

The platform's growth challenge is primarily a supply-side problem rather than a demand-side problem.

Improving restaurant retention and expanding outlet supply is likely to generate higher business impact than customer discounting or promotional campaigns.
